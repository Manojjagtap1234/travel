Forts

Harihar Fort

Harihar Fort (Harshagad) is a historic Nashik fort with unique rock-cut steps. It offers stunning views and is a popular destination for visitors.

Explore
Anjineri Fort

Anjaneri Fort is a historic fort in Nashik, linked to the birthplace of Lord Hanuman. It offers scenic views and is popular among trekkers and pilgrims.

Explore
Dhodap Fort

Dhodap Fort, a 4,829-foot peak in Nashik, is the second-highest fort in the Sahyadri mountains.

Temples

Brahmagiri Temple

Brahmagiri (1298 meters) is to the west of Trimbakeshwar, offering a commanding view of the Jawhar Mokhada region of Thane district. Trimbakeshwar is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva.

Explore
Trimbakeshwar

Shri Trimbakeshwar Temple is located about 28 km from Nashik, near the Brahmagiri mountain.

Explore
Swaminarayan Temple

The Shri Swaminarayan Mandir in Nashik is renowned for its architecture and devotion to Krishna.
