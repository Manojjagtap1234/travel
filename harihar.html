Harihar Fort

About Harihar Fort

A Legacy Carved in Stone Embark on a journey back in time as you explore Harihar Fort. Built during the Seuna (Yadava) dynasty, the fort stands as a proud reminder of a bygone era. Climb the legendary 80-degree inclined steps, a true test of your physical prowess, and be rewarded with breathtaking panoramic vistas of the surrounding landscape. Explore the ancient temples dedicated to Lord Shiva and Hanuman, offering a glimpse into the spiritual significance of the fort. Unravel the mysteries of the fort's fortifications, including the watchtowers and the narrow passage that leads to the summit, a testament to the strategic brilliance of its architects.

Adventure Awaits For the thrill-seeker, Harihar Fort offers an exhilarating experience. The climb itself is an adventure, demanding physical fitness and a sense of determination. As you ascend the steps, feel the rush of adrenaline and the crisp mountain air invigorate your senses. Once at the summit, marvel at the panoramic views of the lush green valleys, the glistening Trimbakeshwar Shiva temple, and the vast expanse of the Sahyadri range.

A Haven for Nature Lovers Harihar Fort is not just about history and adventure. Immerse yourself in the beauty of nature as you explore the surrounding forests. Breathe in the fresh air, listen to the symphony of birdsong, and witness the diverse flora and fauna that thrive in this verdant haven. Spot vibrant butterflies flitting through the air, or catch a glimpse of shy langurs swinging through the trees. The serenity of the natural surroundings provides a welcome contrast to the historical significance of the fort.

How to Reach

From Nashik bus stand, you can take a bus to Trimbakeshwar, and from there, you can hire a cab or use local transport to reach the base village of Nirgudpada. The journey from the bus stand to the base village takes approximately 2 hours.

Where to Stay

There are several options to stay in Trimbakeshwar, including budget hotels and guest houses. You can also stay in Nashik, which offers a wider range of accommodation options, from budget to luxury hotels.

Resources to Go There

  • Private vehicle: The most convenient option
  • Bus: Regular buses from Nashik to Trimbakeshwar
  • Taxi or local transport: Available from Trimbakeshwar to the base village
